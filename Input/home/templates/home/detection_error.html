Capture Tips for a Smooth Experience

To ensure your face is recognized perfectly, here are some quick tips to keep in mind while capturing your photo:

Lighting Matters!

Lighting can affect how clearly your face shows. Here's what to avoid:

  • Dim lighting:** Makes your features hard to see.
  • Harsh shadows:** Creates uneven lighting across your face.
  • Backlight:** Makes your face appear dark.

Aim for:

  • Bright, even lighting:** Natural light from a window is ideal.

By following these simple tips, you'll get a clear face capture that works best with our application.
